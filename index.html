Minerva Down

The world is difficult enough to understand before you begin seeing the systems underneath it.

Ryan is caught between unstable reality, astral technology, and an unseen architecture that may be rewriting more than his perception. What begins as a struggle to understand what is happening becomes a confrontation with the machinery beneath the world—and the intelligence moving through it.

Minerva Down is a LitRPG-adjacent, mythic science-fiction serial about identity, memory, transcendence, and the cost of seeing clearly.

About the author

Writing from the edge of the system.

exSifting is a speculative-fiction author drawn to the places where technical systems, mythology, consciousness, and ordinary human lives collide.

The stories tend to begin with something familiar—a network, a memory, a dream, a family, a machine—and then follow the fracture until the hidden infrastructure becomes visible.

This site is the public-facing home for those stories. The more questionable materials remain one layer deeper.
